We Know Talent Management
We get it. Engaging and retaining talent isn’t just hard in today’s competitive marketplace - it can feel downright impossible. When it comes to an engaged workforce, there are no one-size-fits-all solutions. It was only through our research that it all came back to one fundamental truth: human connection drives engagement. Connection to our peers, to our managers, and most importantly, to the work we do.

Avenue is dedicated to connecting these dots. Our expert facilitators, science-backed leadership tools and proprietary platform gives employees the insight, courage, and guidance they need to maximize their impact within your organization.
Root Causes of Career Apathy
Untapped Opportunities
Among employees who leave a company, more than half do so because of lack of job fit or mobility. The worst part? It’s not due to the lack of opportunities within the company, but rather proactively creating what they want.
Lack of Action
Charting a career path can feel challenging at best. The process can feel hard and complex, causing employees to stop before they even start. Comfort zones are hard to break without the right guidance and support.
Inefficient Support
Even when employees are presented with the right opportunity, career mobility is halted when they don’t have the right managerial support. Worse, they don’t have the tools or right connections to support their own career growth.

A New Approach to Career Development
Career Advantage Program
A half-day in-person program with a 45-day follow-up plan, the Career Advantage shows employees what it means to have meaningful career conversations, who they should be connecting with, and how engaging thoughtfully with the right people can lead to work with meaning.
Career Builder Program
Designed for high performers and future executives, the Career Builder program is a one-day tailored training program that allows employees to explore their strengths and career hopes, identify their ultimate goals, and chart a path within your organization to get there.
