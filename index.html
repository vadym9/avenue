Root Causes of Career Apathy
Untapped Opportunities
Among employees who leave a company, more than half do so because of lack of job fit or mobility. The worst part? It’s not due to the lack of opportunities within the company, but rather proactively creating what they want.
Lack of Action
Charting a career path can feel challenging at best. The process can feel hard and complex, causing employees to stop before they even start. Comfort zones are hard to break without the right guidance and support.
Inefficient Support
Even when employees are presented with the right opportunity, career mobility is halted when they don’t have the right managerial support. Worse, they don’t have the tools or right connections to support their own career growth.
